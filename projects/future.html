The Future

Design information for 2016 summit

The challenge of the brief was to design information and an identity for the upcoming summit, that depicts and discusses the “Six Critical Drivers of Global Change” as stated in Al Gore’s book “The Future“. This was to carefully illustrate the convergence of three chapters in the book.

Solution

The solution was to design a wall chart that demonstrates the interaction of only three selected drivers of change. Depicting the influence of one chapter onto the other. This notion was presented through a time-line... displaying the
