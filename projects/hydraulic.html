Hydraulic Fracturing

A Government Educational Program

Hydraulic Fracturing (Fracking) is a process of extracting gas from shale rocks deep beneath the Earth. The purpose of this campaign was to communicate the good and bas aspect of Hydraulic Fracturing.

Solution

This was designed to be an activist campaign, supporting the “idea” of Hydraulic Fracturing. However, as a process we believe that it causes more harm than good to local communities. The notion within the campaign is designed to be tender and yet; aggressive simultaneously, by which the colours, type and images shared a contrasting relation between each other; therefore, creating a consistant tone of voice.
