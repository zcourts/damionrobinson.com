About

I am passionate young designer who is on a sandwich year taking in as much experience I can, learning and being a valuable asset to the company I work for.

I love everything that has to do with Graphic design; Packaging, web design, Industrial design and I feel a true devotion to advertising and commercial work. As an intern I have already gain experience working whilst studying on a BA at London College of communication. I don’t take criticism and feedback personally I love receiving them more like, it’s only way one can development and become better! I love working with people, especially if they have the same passion I have for what they do.


Philosophy


Design creates ideas, ideas that encourages the public to create change, “design must primarily serve the people”, whilst acting as aesthetic piece of art. A designer is responsible for change in world. Creating a form that function as well in its contemporary holdings.

Critical Mass

A news paper which was built on Collaborations.

Critical mass was printed and distributed throughout the UAL campus and across five selected areas in London. A Project which was built on collaboration and multiple disciplines each page has a different story to tell, selected by the students.

Make Fracking Transparent

Anti – fracking campaign, which aims to communicate and educate the masses .

Anti – fracking campaign, which aims to communicate and educate the masses of the danger and the after affects of hydraulic fracturing in the UK, (Activist campaign)! We believe that hydraulic causes more harm than good, and the people who are affected by the effects of hydraulic fracturing are the local community, which is why we use humanistic elements to build this campaign.

Typographic Signage within Southwark

How much can a sign say about our city location.

Exploring the typographic signage in Southwark London (the cut), whilst using sound waves to represent each sign and the density of the populated area.

A Wartime Story

A series of postcards created through war story from the time period of WW1-2007 the most contemporary date.

Surrealistic postcards created based on the various war stories found and submitted to the Imperial war museum, Croydon museum, along side pawn shops during the time period of “WW1 & WW2”, exhibited at Ravensbourne university, Viewed by Grayson Perry and colleagues open exhibition to the general public.

Customers Relationship Mangement (CRM)

Branding & Website design

Designing and creating a branding scheme for a customer relationship management group, a simplistic scheme that has been carried across the entire website and mobile application design (UX/UI).

Therapeutic night-light

Therapeutic night-light for children who finds it particular hard to go to sleep at night.

Using the psychological and therapeutic characteristic of the colour orange to create night-light for children who find it hard to go to sleep, branded as tang-night, inspired by the tangerine fruit, a fruit that has been used in Greek medicine for headaches, stomach aches, nausea, etc.
