Cultural Identity Crisis

Cultural Identity Crisis is a branding and identity, made to represent the cultural diversity of art and design located in eight cities across the UK.
This brand aims to communicate that diversity in multiple ways; using shapes, colours and imagery based on the art and design sceneries within the cities.
